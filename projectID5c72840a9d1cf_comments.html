A long piece of Scottish historical fiction, somewhat similar to Waverley by Walter Scott, although in his preface, the author denies this is intentional.

The scans are from a one volume reprint from 1886; the first edition appeared in three volumes in 1827, 13 years after Waverley. Scans for these are here: Volume I, II, III.
